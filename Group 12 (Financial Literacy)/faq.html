Frequently Asked Questions

Do you give certificate of completion ?

No, the purpose of our website Financial Literacy 101 is the give our users financial literacy knowledge, skills and confidence to make effective and well inform financial decisions.

Are your services free ?

Most of our content is free because we aim to educate our clientel so we can have an economical and social stable self sufficient society.

How often do you update the information in your website?

We frequently update our information so that our clientel get the latest information.

Do you draw business plans for your subscribers?

Perhaps in the near future we will provide such a service but as of now we don't draw any business plans or anything of that nature. To get an idea of how a business plan should be structured follow this link.

Do I need to be a subscriber to get your services?

To reep full benefits and knowledge from our website financial literacy 101 you must indeed be a subscriber. Otherwise we offer our services to everyone who set their eyes on our url.

Do you provide fund to small businesses?

No, we do not fund small business but rather inform small medium and micro enterprises (SMMEs) of ways they can be funded such as crowdfunding

Where can I get financial help to grow my business ?

There are numerous way to get financial help to grow your business. Before you can consider a loan first try these government programs which intend to help and groom particularly small businesses.

  • National Empowerment Fund (NEF) - support Broad-based Black Economic Empowerment (BBBEE)
  • Industrial Development Corporation (IDC) - is a national development finance institution set up to promote economic growth and industrial development.
  • Finfind - links SMEs who are seeking finance with appropriate lenders.
  • SEDA (Small Enterprise Development Agency) - provides business development and support services for small enterprises through its national network.
